SugarRun

Run for a healthy life.

About

SugarRun is a Sports App which helps your regular runs become more challenging and interesting, even giving you healthy prizes which help increase your life span.
All you need to do is to run the set course at the set pace and pick up as many fruits on the map as possible.
By collecting fruits, you receive points which add up to actual discounts and prizes at selected health-food stores.
SugarRun keeps track of your fitness level and progress and increases the difficulty level as you progress, thus enabling you to become more fit and to earn more points.
